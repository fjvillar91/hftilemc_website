Welcome to H&F Tile Marble Corp.

We specialize in the installation of tile, ceramic, marble, stone, and cement. We take great pride in our abilities to create, maintenance, and renew bathrooms and kitchens. We work both commercially and residentially. Contact us for a free quote!

Our History

The company was started in 2005 by Hugo J. Villar. The company has prospered along within the greater New York City metropolitan area. H&F has tiled Restaurants to the finest Condominiums, as well as serving the tile needs of homeowners throughout the five boroughs.

Our Staff

Our staff is both experienced and creative. In their capable hands they turn formless, lifeless stone into elegant vibrant pieces of art. More importantly our staff has been time tested and can handle any project thrown their way.
